Privacy Policy for Unnati (MP)

Thank you for using Unnati (MP), the mobile application for collecting crop data related to GIS. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect:

1.1. Personal Information:

When you use Unnati (MP), we may collect the following personal information:

  • Mobile Number: We collect your mobile number to uniquely identify you as a user of our app and to provide you with relevant updates and notifications.
  • Email Address: We collect your email address to communicate with you regarding your account, provide support, and send important information related to Unnati (MP).

1.2. Non-Personal Information:

In addition to personal information, we may collect non-personal information such as usage data, device information, and analytics about your interaction with the app. This information is collected to improve our services, enhance the user experience, and ensure the app's functionality.

2. Use of Information:

2.1. Personal Information:

We use your personal information, specifically your mobile number and email address, for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: To create and manage your user account on Unnati (MP).
  • Communication: To send important updates, notifications, and other relevant information regarding the app and its services.
  • Support: To respond to your inquiries, provide assistance, and resolve any issues you may encounter.
  • Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

2.2. Non-Personal Information:

Non-personal information is used in an aggregated and anonymous form to analyze app usage patterns, improve our services, and enhance the user experience.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure:

We never share your personal information (mobile number and email address) with any third parties without your explicit consent, unless required by law or in compliance with legal processes.

4. Data Security:

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We implement appropriate security measures to safeguard the confidentiality and integrity of your data.

5. Data Retention:

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

6. Third-Party Links:

Unnati (MP) may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third-party sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites or services before providing any personal information.

7. Children's Privacy:

Unnati (MP) is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to remove the information.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy:

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting a prominent notice on our website or through other communication channels.

9. Contact Us:
