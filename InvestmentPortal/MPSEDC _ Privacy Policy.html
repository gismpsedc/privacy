Privacy Policy for IT Investment Portal

The IT Investment Portal, developed under the Madhya Pradesh IT, ITeS, and ESDM Investment Promotion Policy 2016 and Madhya Pradesh IT, ITeS, and ESDM Investment Promotion Policy 2023, values your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information collected through its platform. This privacy policy outlines the types of information we collect, its intended use, and the measures taken to secure your data, in alignment with industry standards.

Information Collection and Usage

When you interact with the IT Investment Portal, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, contact details (phone number and email), business or investment-related details, and other relevant data provided during registration or while using our services.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and usage statistics to improve user experience and portal performance.

The collected information is used to:

  • Facilitate investment opportunities and provide you with the services outlined under the 2016 and Madhya Pradesh IT, ITeS, and ESDM Investment Promotion Policy, 2023.
  • Enhance the portal's functionality and tailor services to user needs.
  • Communicate important updates and respond to user queries or requests.

Information Sharing

Your personal information will not be sold, rented, or shared with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • To facilitate services or benefits under the investment policy, as explicitly agreed by you.
  • To comply with legal or regulatory requirements, or as required by government authorities.

We may share anonymized and aggregated data for statistical purposes or policy improvements without disclosing individual user identities.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The portal uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience and gather insights about user behavior. Cookies help us:

  • Remember your preferences and improve website performance.
  • Analyze portal usage to refine its features and services.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of the portal may not function optimally without them.

Data Security

The IT Investment Portal implements industry-standard security measures, including but not limited to:

  • Data Encryption: Ensures secure transmission of information between your device and the portal.
  • Access Controls: Limits access to your personal information to authorized personnel only.
  • Regular Security Audits: Identifies and addresses vulnerabilities to maintain data integrity.

Despite our efforts, no online system can guarantee absolute security. Users are advised to exercise caution and ensure their credentials are protected.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or correct your personal information.
  • Withdraw consent for certain types of data processing.
  • Request the deletion of your personal data, subject to applicable laws and retention policies.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details provided in the "Contact Us" section below.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in the portal's functionality, legal requirements, or industry standards. Any updates will be posted on this page, and significant changes will be communicated via email or portal notifications.
