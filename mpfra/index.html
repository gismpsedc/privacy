Privacy Policy – MPFRA Web & Mobile Application

This Privacy Policy describes how the MPFRA Application (Web and Mobile), developed by MPSeDC and MAP_IT, collects, uses, and protects user information. This policy is published in compliance with the data safety and privacy requirements of Google Play.

The application is intended for official government use to support field verification, mapping, monitoring, and related departmental processes for improving citizen services.

1. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information (User-Provided)

The application may collect the following information when provided by authorized users:

  • Name, username, and login credentials
  • Mobile number and email ID
  • Departmental or role-based information
  • Information submitted for official workflows (e.g., land data, operational workflow)

This data is collected for authentication, audit tracking, and operational purposes only.

b) Location Information

The application may collect precise GPS location when required for field activities such as mapping or verification. Location data is collected only after user permission.

c) Device and Technical Information

For system performance and security, the application may collect:

  • Device model and operating system
  • Application version
  • Error logs and usage data

The application may use trusted third-party services such as:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Crashlytics (for crash reporting and performance monitoring)

2. How We Use the Information

Collected information is used to:

  • Enable secure login and authentication
  • Support departmental workflows and field verification
  • Improve application performance and reliability
  • Maintain audit logs and system security
  • Support government planning and citizen service delivery

No personal data is used for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties. Information may be shared only:

  • When required by law or government process
  • With authorized government departments
  • With trusted technical service providers under confidentiality obligations

Aggregated or non-sensitive data may be published for public information as per government policies.

4. Data Retention

  • User-provided data is retained as per departmental and legal requirements.
  • Technical and log data may be retained for up to 24 months for monitoring and security.
  • Data may be stored longer where required for audit or compliance purposes.

Users may request permissible data correction through the official helpdesk.

5. User Permissions

The application may request permissions strictly for functional purposes:

  • Location (GPS-based mapping/verification)
  • Camera (capturing field images/documents)
  • Storage (uploading supporting documents)

6. Data Security

Appropriate technical and administrative safeguards are implemented to protect data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. Access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

7. Children’s Privacy

This application is intended for authorized government users and is not designed for children under 13 years of age. The application does not knowingly collect data from children.

8. User Control and Opt-Out

Users may stop data collection by:

  • Uninstalling the mobile application, or
  • Discontinuing use of the web application.

For data-related requests:
Email: helpdesk.vanmitra@mp.gov.in

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect system or regulatory changes. Updated versions will be published within the application or official platforms.

10. Contact Us

For any privacy-related queries:
Helpdesk Email: helpdesk.vanmitra@mp.gov.in
