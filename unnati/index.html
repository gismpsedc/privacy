Privacy Policy for Unnati (MP)

Thank you for using Unnati (MP), the mobile application for collecting crop data related to GIS. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you use Unnati (MP), we may collect the following personal information:

  • Mobile Number: To uniquely identify you and provide relevant updates and notifications.
  • Email Address: To communicate regarding your account, provide support, and send important information.

1.2 Non-Personal Information

We may collect usage data, device information, and analytics to improve our services and enhance user experience.

2. Use of Information

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Management
  • Communication & Notifications
  • Customer Support
  • Legal Compliance

2.2 Non-Personal Information

Used in aggregated and anonymous form to analyze usage patterns and improve the app.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent unless required by law.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Data Retention

Your information is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

6. Third-Party Links

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites linked within the app.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated through appropriate channels.
